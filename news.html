November 2011 New update!

The world’s first Earthship school is almost complete!

In October 2011, a crew of over 30 people, including the American architect and creator of Earthship, Michael Reynolds, worked for ten days to build 2 classrooms and teach locals the Earthship building methods. Please see our Earthship page for the latest photos from the build. More classrooms are being added along the way, bringing us ever closer to the vision of The Goderich Waldorf School, Sierra Leone.
