Dear Friends,

After a very challenging time with the Ebola outbreak and the closure of all schools in Sierra Leone, the Goderich Waldorf School finally reopened in April 2015. The children are thriving once again, thanks to the support of their families, teachers and community of Rokel village. Following is the school’s Annual Report 2015 from the school’s director, Mr Shannon Kandoh, where you can read more about the school. You can also read more news in the current school newsletter.

May 2016 bring peace and prosperity to our friends in Sierra Leone.

Pema Clark

The Dhana Trust (UK)
for The Goderich Waldorf School
